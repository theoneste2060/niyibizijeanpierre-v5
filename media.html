Official Biography

NIYIBIZI Jean Pierre is a Rwandan Geotechnical Engineer, Researcher, and AI Innovator dedicated to integrating artificial intelligence into civil and agricultural engineering. As the Owner & CEO of GEO-HOME SERVICES LTD, he leads innovative projects that combine soil mechanics, data analysis, and sustainable construction to build climate-resilient systems for Africa's future. His research focuses on predictive soil modeling, slope stability, and data-driven solutions for sustainable infrastructure. NIYIBIZI Jean Pierre has published multiple peer-reviewed papers and frequently speaks at regional and international conferences about the intersection of AI, geotechnics, and sustainable development.
